Welcome to Ahltee!

Our goal is to track changes made to the various Myst novels. Anyone can view the changes we've found, but you'll need an account to participate. Submissions from registered users must be approved by a mod before updating publicly.

The information gathered here is organized into Scraps, which are general descriptions of changes made. Scraps include a brief summar, the applicable novel, and various tags to make it easier to find similar item. You can use existing tags and create your own.

Scraps also contain Snippets, which show the text exactly as it appears in the novels. For any given Scrap, there exists excatly one Snippet per edition of the applicable book.

Ready to get started? After you register, head on over to Guidelines to review our rules and helpful tips. Then visit Scraps to view, edit, and add Scraps of your own.
