Welcome to Ahltee!
The Myst novel change tracker

Ahltee is the D'ni word for branches. Our purpose is to record and share changes made to the (currently three) Myst novels. Anyone can view the changes posted here, but you'll need an account to participate. Submissions from registered users must be approved by a moderator before displaying publicly.

The information gathered here is organized into Scraps, which are general descriptions of changes made. Scraps include a brief summary, the applicable novel, and various tags to make it easier to find similar items. You can use existing tags or create your own.

Scraps also contain Snippets, which show the text exactly as it appears in the novels. When you open a Scrap, you'll find one Snippet for every edition of that particular novel.

Ready to get started? After you register, head on over to Guidelines to review our rules and helpful tips. Then visit Scraps to view, edit, and add Scraps of your own.
