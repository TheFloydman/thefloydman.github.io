Instructions
Use your cursor or touch the screen to select a point, then select another point to connect or disconnect them. The up arrow converts the displayed decimal number in Villein, and the down arrow converts the displayed Villein number into decimal.
Just like in the video game, the panel will self-correct if you enter a number on the panel incorrectly.
All layers 3 and above (numbers above 1023) do not appear in-game and are extrapolated based on the author's best guess.
